## What this video shows

A walkthrough of a typical project structure: listing files, reading the README, and exploring the source directory.

## What you will see

1. Listing the project root contents.
2. Reading the README for build instructions.
3. Exploring the source directory modules.

## Why this tape exists

This tape has intentional timing overlaps in steps 2, 4, and 6 — the narration runs longer than the pause allows, causing audio collisions. It serves as a demo for the playback TUI editor: open it with `npm run playback:edit -- studio/example/` and use <kbd>h</kbd>/<kbd>l</kbd> to nudge the audio clips until the overlaps resolve.

## What comes next

Once you've fixed the timing in the TUI, press <kbd>s</kbd> to save and <kbd>r</kbd> to re-run the pipeline. The updated video will have clean, non-overlapping narration.
