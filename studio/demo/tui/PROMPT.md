## What this video shows

A walkthrough of the playback TUI editor, demonstrating the core workflow: opening a tape with timing issues, fixing overlapping narration with audio nudge controls, and saving the result.

## What you will see

1. Opening the TUI with a demo tape that has intentional overlaps.
2. Navigating to affected steps and seeing the overlap warnings.
3. Using <kbd>h</kbd>/<kbd>l</kbd> to nudge audio clips until overlaps resolve.
4. Saving the corrected timing back to `tape.yaml`.
5. Browsing the tape picker and keybinding reference.

## What you will need

- playback installed (`npm run setup`)
- The demo tape built (`npm run playback:tape -- studio/example/tape`)
